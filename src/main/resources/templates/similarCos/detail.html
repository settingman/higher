A gel-to-foam cleanser created to hydrate, cleanse and help prevent against oxidative stress. The formulation addresses the impact of internal and external aggressors on our skin, seeking to protect the skin’s precious skin barrier, keeping it hydrated, whilst also clearing external aggressors.

Described as Magic in a bottle in Consumer Trials - the hydrating gel texture turns to a delicate foam when combined with water to cleanse effectively without leaving the skin dry and taught thanks to natural surfactants and a pH level (pH5.3) close to the skin’s own. The precious skin barrier remains protected.

Fragrance chords of Lemon, Lavender, Eucalyptus and Lemon Myrtle guarantee a refreshing start to the day.

How to use:

Suitable for all skin types. Apply 1-2 pumps to damp skin and massage onto the face in a gentle circular motion to create a lather. Rinse with lukewarm water and pat dry.

Ideal for shaving too.

When double cleansing with Nourishing Cleansing Balm, warm 2-3 Wildsmith Skin spoons of Nourishing Cleansing Balm on fingertips, smooth on to a dry skin, massage for 60 seconds, add tepid water for the balm to turn to a milk. Rinse well or remove with warm damp Wildsmith Skin muslin cloths and pat dry.
